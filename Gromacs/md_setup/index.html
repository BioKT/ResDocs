Running a simulation in Gromacs

These brief instructions are a general guide to how to run a simple MD simulation using Gromacs. In this case we are simulating the alanine dipeptide using Gromacs 2018 but things should not change too much for other recent versions of the software. You must have a working version installed in your machine. First of all, you will have to download the compressed files in alaTB_files.tar.gz to your computer and extract them typing 

tar -xvf ala_TB_files.tar.gz

in your terminal.
