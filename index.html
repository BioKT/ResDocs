BioKT Research Toolbox

This is the documentation website for the BioKT group at the University of the Basque Country and the Donostia International Physics Center. We are a team lead by Xabier Lopez and David De Sancho that focuses on the study of the conformational dynamics of biomolecules with computational methods, in particular molecular simulation and quantum mechanical calculations. You can find more information about the work we do in our website. Much of the code we write is hosted in Github. In this website you will find information about a number of common tools that we use in our research.
